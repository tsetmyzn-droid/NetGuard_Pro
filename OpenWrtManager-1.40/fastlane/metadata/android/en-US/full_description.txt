This app allows you to view the status of your OpenWRT devices using LuCI commands.
LuCI must be installed on OpenWRT device.
You can connect to multiple devices in the app and select which info is shown.
Currently mainly viewing data and status is available.

<b>Available actions:</b>
<ul>
  <li>Reboot the device. (Devices Page)</li>
  <li>Disconnect a selected Wi-Fi client from the list (Long press).</li>
  <li>Restart network interface.</li>
</ul>

It is recommended to enable HTTPS for LuCI on your device.
